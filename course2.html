SITXHRM003- Lead and manage people:

  • This course, SITHKOP006 - Plan catering for events or functions, is designed for individuals aiming to acquire expertise in planning catering services for various events or functions.
  • Throughout this course, participants will explore comprehensive aspects of event catering planning, including menu development, logistics, budgeting, and coordination.
  • Experienced and qualified instructors lead the course, dedicating themselves to assisting participants in gaining proficiency in planning catering services. They provide clear explanations, practical examples, and personalized feedback to support the learning journey.
  • Participants will engage in practical exercises and real-world scenarios within a supportive and interactive learning environment. Group activities, collaborative planning, and hands-on applications will be integral to reinforcing the skills needed for planning catering services.
  • Upon completion of the course, participants will have the confidence to plan catering services for a variety of events or functions. They will be equipped with essential planning skills, a solid understanding of catering concepts, and the ability to approach catering challenges with proficiency.
  • Whether you are pursuing catering planning for personal enrichment or professional advancement, this course provides an excellent starting point for your journey in the field of event catering.
